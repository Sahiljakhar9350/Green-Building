Key Benefits

Green buildings reduce energy costs, improve indoor air quality, support eco-friendly practices, and create healthier environments. They can help in combating climate change by reducing carbon footprints.

How It Works

Green building incorporates techniques like passive solar design, rainwater harvesting, renewable energy sources, and high-performance insulation to create efficient and sustainable spaces.
