What is Green Building?

Green building refers to designing, constructing, and operating buildings in ways that are environmentally responsible and resource-efficient throughout a building's life cycle. From the initial planning to demolition, green building practices aim to reduce the overall impact on human health and the natural environment.

Energy Efficiency

Green buildings minimize energy consumption and use renewable energy sources like solar and wind power.

Water Conservation

These buildings integrate systems that conserve water and use it more efficiently.

Eco-friendly Materials

They make use of materials that are sustainable, non-toxic, and often locally sourced.
